Browser History

The top banner on Bandcamp, along with all the links on the page, will turn green or red based on your Chrome browsing history. This helps you identify if you've visited these pages before.


  Play History

Tracks will display in one of ten shades of red, based on your play history.

Use the following buttons to manage your data. All data is lost when the extension is uninstalled.




  Tracks In Common

See how many tracks you have in common with other users by hover over their thumbnail in the track or album view.

Bandcamp will limit your traffic, if you open to many tabs at the same time.

  External Buttons

Buttons appear for Soundcloud and Buy Music Club. Furthermore, arrow keys on your keyboard allow you to navigate playlists on BMC.
